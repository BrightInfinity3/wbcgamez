SoloTerra

How to Play

Goal

  • Place the 10 of Diodes on its Foundation Stack to win.
  • Your score reflects how many cards you saved.

Layout

  • 40-card deck (ranks 1–10), 4 suits, 6 tableau columns.
  • Only the top card of each tableau column starts face-up.
  • The remaining cards form the Stock Pile in the top-left.
  • An Active Pile and 4 Foundation Stacks also sit in the top row and start empty.

Controls

  • Click a card to select it, then drag the card or click a destination to move it.
  • Double-click a card to auto-send it to its Foundation Stack.

Building Foundations

  • Each Foundation Stack builds up by suit from 1 – 10.
  • Once a card is placed on a Foundation Stack, it cannot be removed.

Building Tableaus

  • Tableau columns build down by rank with a suit hierarchy.
  • A higher ranked suit cannot be placed below a lower ranked one.
  • Suit hierarchy: Diodes > Prisms > Blades > Combiners
  • You can move a face-up card (and all cards below it) to another tableau column.
  • Only a 10 can be placed on an empty tableau column.

Stock Pile

  • Click the Stock Pile to draw one card to the top of the Active Pile.
  • The top Active Pile card can be placed on a tableau column or Foundation Stack.
  • When the Stock Pile is empty, click it again to recycle the cards.

Scoring

  • Your score calculates saved cards — those not placed on Foundation Stacks:
  • Score = Saved Prisms × 1 + Saved Blades × 2 + Saved Combiners × 3
  • A score of 60 is a perfect game (all cards saved). Higher is better!
