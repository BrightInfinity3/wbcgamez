How to Play

Goal

Get as close to 30 as possible without going over.

Setup

Each player is dealt 3 cards from a standard deck.

Action moves clockwise with the dealer's turn last.

Card Values

Aces are worth 1.

Numbered cards are worth their face value.

Jacks, Queens, and Kings are worth 10.

Gameplay

On your turn, choose one of two actions:

Draw — Take a card. Action moves to the next player.

Stay — Lock in your hand for the round.

Winning

The player closest to 30 wins.

If tied, the player with more cards wins.

Still tied? The player who last drew a card wins.
